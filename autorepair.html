Auto Repair Services

We offer General Repair and Maintenance services for domestic and foreign automobiles. In addition we service Brakes, Exhaust, Suspension, Heating and Air Conditioning, Diagnostics, and any other repair service you might need. We can also arrange to have your car towed to the shop for you. We have over 30 years of experience so we know what needs to be done and how to do it right so you can stay on the road.

We have one service bay and one lift so we can do what it takes to get the job done. In 2019 we will be adding three new bays and another lift so that we can get you in and out even quicker than we can now.



We have a strong relationship with Federated Auto Parts and work with them to get most of the parts we use here. However, we do have connections with many other part stores and dealerships so we are able to get any part we need as quickly as possible. We are also happy to work with OEM or Aftermarket parts to give your car that custom feel and performance.
